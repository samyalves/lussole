OUR STORY

In the heart of Milan, Lussole emerged as a luxury sunglasses brand, fusing sophistication and charm.
In the early 20th century, visionary Giovanna Bianchari set out to create a sunglass that embodied Italy’s elegance and the client’s necessities. Our sunglasses are created with premium materials, crafted frames that were both stylish and functional, and lenses with impeccable UV protection.
Lussole grew to encompass a legacy of uniqueness, and a testament to Italian craftsmanship.

  • Premium Materials

FAQ

Can I order a Lussole as a prescription sunglasses??
Unfortunately, Lussole is not available as a prescription sunglasses option.
Are your sunglasses UV-protected?
Our sunglasses are designed with UV protection to shield your eyes from harmful ultraviolet rays. You can confidently enjoy the sun while knowing that our sunglasses provide the necessary protection to keep your eyes safe.
What is the warranty on your sunglasses?
We offer a 5 year warranty on our sunglasses, which covers manufacturing defects and ensures your satisfaction with our product.
